Overview

How Konsume works

Konsume is built for the millions of us who cook, with multiple vendors competing for your order, prices exactly as they are in the market, and a way to pool with people online to buy in bulk, split, and get more while spending less.

Set your location and discover sellers

Step 01

Drop your location

Konsume guarantees market pricing on every listing. But it goes further, multiple vendors compete for your order openly, and you can negotiate directly. A vendor lists ₦1,800? Offer ₦1,600. If they accept, it's a done deal. Konsume brings the negotiation culture of the market to your phone.

Buy alone or join a pool

Step 02

Pool with others

Choose to buy individually at market price, or join a pool. When enough people pool together to hit the bulk threshold, everyone gets wholesale pricing and Konsume handles the buying, splitting, and delivery. Every product on Konsume has a pool option. See how many people have joined, how close the pool is to filling, and share your unique link to speed it up.

We handle buying, splitting, delivery

Step 03

You buy. We carry

After you order or your pool fills, Konsume's team collects from the market at the price you saw on the app, splits pooled orders by contribution, and delivers directly to you. Individual orders land same day. Pool orders land once split and dispatched.
